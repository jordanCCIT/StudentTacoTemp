Slow Cooked Carnitas

Fat-Poached Carnitas Ingredients: *3 pounds of boneless pork shoulder or uncured ham. *.5 cups of liquid fat. *15 cloves of garlic. *1 thinly sliced lemon. *Coarse salt. *Black Pepper. Instructions: 1. Heat oven to 250 degrees. 2. Cube pork shoulder into 3 inch chucks. 3. Salt and pepper the cubes evenly. 4. Tightly stuff cubes into a deep baking dish. 5. Stuff garlic and lemon between cubes. 6. Cover cubes in liquified fat. 7. Bake for 5 hours. 8. Remove from oven and cover with foil. 9. Remove pork from dish and spread out onto a baking pan. 10. Turn on broiler. 11. Broil pork until it's a bit blackened and crispy (around 4-6 minutes). 12. Remove from oven and shread with fork.
